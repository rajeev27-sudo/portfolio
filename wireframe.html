My Wireframes

Dive into my wireframing work, showcasing both digital and hand-drawn approaches to design ideation and structure.

Digital Wireframes: Low Fidelity

These wireframes illustrate the more refined stages of design, focusing on detailed layout, information hierarchy, and interactive elements created using professional tools.

Project Context: Library Stores & Dog Walking App

These digital wireframes were part of a project to redesign on a library and dog walker's UI Design. The goal was to simplify the transfer process and improve account overview accessibility. I focused on intuitive navigation and clear visual hierarchy.

Hand-Drawn Wireframes: Rapid Ideation

My hand-drawn wireframes represent the initial brainstorming and rapid ideation phases. They help in quickly visualizing concepts and flows before moving to digital tools.

Hand Drawn Wireframe

These sketches were used during the early discovery phase for a new online learning platform. I explored various user flows for course enrollment and content consumption, prioritizing speed and flexibility in concept generation.
