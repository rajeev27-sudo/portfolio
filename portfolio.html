About Me

As a passionate UX Designer, I thrive at the intersection of human behavior and digital innovation. My journey into UX began with a fascination for understanding why people interact with technology the way they do, stemming from my background in Master's in Computer Application and a Google UX Design Professional Certificate.

I specialize in transforming complex problems into intuitive, user-friendly experiences. My core strengths lie in user research, information architecture, wireframing, prototyping, and rigorous usability testing. I'm dedicated to designing solutions that not only meet business objectives but genuinely delight users and simplify their lives.

What truly excites me is the process of discovery and iteration. I believe the best designs emerge from a deep empathy for users, continuous learning, and collaborative feedback. I'm always eager to dive into new challenges, learn emerging design methodologies, and contribute to products that make a real difference.
